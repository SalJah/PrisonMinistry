FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about TRULINCS, CorrLinks, and SmartInmates.

Verify with the inmate that they entered your email address properly:
• Pay special attention to the spelling of addresses and domain names (.com, .net, etc.).
• Once verified, check your junk mail folder for filtered messages.

Check the email date as the invitation is valid for 10 days. If expired, the inmate must re-add the request.
Contact CorrLinks support at https://www.corrlinks.com/Help.aspx.
You should choose "I don't have an identification code or the one I have doesn't work" from the dropdown, enter your email, and click Go.

Ensure the inmate is at a facility offering TRULINCS and has access to Public Messaging. Contract facilities may not have CorrLinks.
Verify your CorrLinks box for messages sent. Contact support if necessary:
https://www.corrlinks.com/Help.aspx.
Approved locations: https://www.bop.gov/locations/list.jsp.

If blocked, the inmate must submit a request to the local institution's Trust Fund Office to remove the block. Once removed, you will receive a new system-generated message from CorrLinks.

• SmartInmates: Most messages are delivered instantly, but some facilities may delay delivery.

SmartInmates: You may keep a message on your account indefinitely. Messages no longer wanted can be deleted anytime.

SmartInmates: No daily limit. Each message costs one message credit (50 cents) if sender has none.

• CorrLinks: Limited to 13,000 characters (approx. 2 pages). Text only, no attachments.
• SmartInmates: Limited to 30,000 characters including spaces (approx. 8-10 pages).
